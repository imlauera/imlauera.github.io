Cortar audio

September 08, 2024
Ffmpeg

This works for me

ffmpeg -ss 60 -i input-audio.m4a -t 15 -c copy output.m4a

  • -ss 60 means, “start from second 60”
  • -t 15 audio output length in seconds.. in this case, 15 seconds..
